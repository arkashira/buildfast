# partner-targets.md

## Partner Integration Roadmap for BuildFast

### Target Integrations

| Partner/API          | Free-Tier Limits                     | Integration Effort | Value-Add (User Job Solved)                                                                 | Affiliate/Revenue-Share |
|----------------------|--------------------------------------|--------------------|----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|--------------------------|
| **Zapier**           | 5 Zaps, 100 tasks/month              | M                  | Automates workflows between BuildFast and other apps, enabling users to connect tools easily. | Yes                      |
| **Airtable**        | 1,200 records/month                  | S                  | Provides a flexible database solution for users to manage their projects and data visually. | Yes                      |
| **Stripe**           | No free tier, but pay-as-you-go     | M                  | Facilitates payment processing for products built on BuildFast, enabling monetization.       | Yes                      |
| **Twilio**           | 5,000 SMS or 1,000 voice minutes     | M                  | Enables communication features (SMS, voice) within applications built on BuildFast.         | Yes                      |
| **Notion**           | 1,000 blocks/month                   | S                  | Allows users to document their projects and collaborate seamlessly within their applications. | No                       |
| **Google Sheets**    | 15 GB storage                        | S                  | Provides a familiar interface for users to manage data and integrate with other tools.       | No                       |
| **Figma**            | 3 Figma files                        | M                  | Enables users to design UI/UX directly within their applications, streamlining the design process. | No                       |
| **Slack**            | 10 integrations, 5 GB storage        | S                  | Facilitates team communication and project updates directly from BuildFast applications.     | Yes                      |

### Rationale for Selection

1. **Zapier**: As a leading automation tool, integrating with Zapier allows users to connect BuildFast with thousands of other applications, enhancing functionality and user experience. The affiliate program can also provide additional revenue opportunities.

2. **Airtable**: This integration offers users a powerful database solution that is easy to use, allowing them to manage their projects and data effectively. The affiliate program can drive additional revenue.

3. **Stripe**: Essential for any platform that enables product monetization, integrating Stripe will allow users to easily set up payment processing. The pay-as-you-go model is attractive for indie hackers.

4. **Twilio**: Communication is key in modern applications. Twilio's integration will enable users to add SMS and voice capabilities, enhancing user engagement and functionality.

5. **Notion**: While it does not have an affiliate program, Notion's integration will help users document their projects and collaborate more effectively, adding significant value to the platform.

6. **Google Sheets**: A familiar tool for many users, integrating Google Sheets allows for easy data management and manipulation, which is crucial for rapid development.

7. **Figma**: Design is a critical aspect of software development. Figma's integration will allow users to design and prototype their applications within BuildFast, streamlining the development process.

8. **Slack**: Integrating Slack will enhance team collaboration and communication, making it easier for users to manage projects and updates directly from their applications. The affiliate program can also provide additional revenue opportunities.

### Conclusion

The integration roadmap focuses on tools that enhance the user experience, streamline workflows, and provide monetization options, with a priority on those that offer affiliate or revenue-sharing opportunities.
